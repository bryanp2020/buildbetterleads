Compliance & Data Policy

Respect for privacy and inboxes is built‑in.

Where do the leads come from?

We compile leads from publicly available sources including funding announcements, company press releases, hiring boards, tech‑stack signals, and verified business directories. Every record is refreshed weekly and contact details are verified prior to delivery.

Is outreach compliant with anti‑spam laws?

Yes. Our data supports compliant B2B outreach under applicable frameworks (e.g., CAN‑SPAM, GDPR legitimate interest, CASL). Always include clear identification, a valid physical address, and an unsubscribe / opt‑out link. Limit cadence, honor opt‑outs promptly, and avoid automated bulk to personal inboxes where restricted.

What permissions do I grant when I subscribe?

By subscribing, you consent to receive transactional emails (order confirmations, access instructions) and periodic product updates or tips. You can unsubscribe from non‑transactional emails anytime via the link in our messages.

Best Practices Checklist
  • • Use business addresses (no personal inboxes)
  • • Personalize by role & signal (funding/hiring)
  • • Include company address & opt‑out link
  • • Respect unsubscribes across systems
  • • Warm up domains & monitor reputation
Data Handling
